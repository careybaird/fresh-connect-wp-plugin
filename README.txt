Connects your blog with the Fresh Cloud platform

== Description ==

The Fresh Connect plugin connects your WordPress website with [Fresh Cloud](https://freshcloud.pro), allowing 1 click logins and powerful statistics.

Some of the features of Fresh Cloud include:

* Create fully cloud hosted websites in a few clicks
* "Hands Free" import your existing websites, done by our team and 100% tested
* One click logins to your WP Admin area
* Full page loading speed monitoring and reports
* Create affiliate websites with Fresh Store

Fresh Connect creates a secure link between your website and your Fresh Cloud account. This allows you to do things like:

* Login to your website with a single click from your Fresh Cloud account
* Monitor and record page load speed automatically
* See your website health & speed reports directly in your Fresh Cloud account
* Be notified of issues, plugin upgrades, WordPress upgrades etc. instantly
* Automate upgrades and common tasks

The Fresh Connect plugin is installed automatically when you use Fresh Cloud. While the plugin is not required, we strongly recommend you use it to give you the extra benefits above.

For support with the Fresh Connect plugin or Fresh Cloud, please [contact our helpdesk](https://freshcloud.pro/contact-us/)

To see the Terms of Use for Fresh Cloud and Fresh Connect [please see our Terms of Use page here](https://freshcloud.pro/terms-of-use/).

== Installation ==

#### Thank you for your interest in the Fresh Connect Plugin.

After downloading the ZIP file of the Fresh Connect plugin,  

1. Log in to the administrator panel.   
2. Go to Plugins Add > New > Upload.     
3. Click "Choose file" ("Browse") and select the downloaded zip file of Fresh Connect plugin.   
4. Click "Install Now" button. 
5. Click "Activate Plugin" button.
